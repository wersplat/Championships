UPA Summer Championship

Group Play Schedule & Format

Dates: July 19–24, 2025

All 16 teams advance to the bracket. Group results determine seeding (1–16).

Format

  • 16 Teams → 4 Groups of 4
  • Round-robin format (3 games per team)
  • All matches are Best-of-1
  • Scoring: Win = 10 pts, Played = 1 pt, Full Roster Bonus = 4 pts
  • Seeding based on total points + tiebreakers (H2H → Point Diff → SOS)

Group Play Schedule

  • Friday, July 19 – Group Play Round 1 (Groups A & B)
  • Saturday, July 20 – Group Play Round 1 (Groups C & D)
  • Sunday, July 21 – Group Play Round 2 (Groups A & B)
  • Monday, July 22 – Group Play Round 2 (Groups C & D)
  • Tuesday, July 23 – Group Play Round 3 (Groups A & B)
  • Wednesday, July 24 – Group Play Round 3 (Groups C & D)

Note:

  • All teams advance to the Double Elimination Bracket (BO3) starting July 25
  • Seeding based on group play results (1 vs 16, 2 vs 15, etc.)
  • Grand Finals is Best-of-5 on July 27
  • Box score screenshots must be submitted after each match
