UPA Summer Championship Format

The UPA Summer Championship is a double elimination tournament. Featuring a hybrid of BYOT and Draft teams. BYOT teams are prequalified through the four season of the Road to $25K Series. Each BYOT team owner has been given option to draft their roster or keep their roster. If BYOT team opts to redraft their roster, their unkept players from registered roster will be returned to the draft pool free to be picked by any team owner during live draft.

Roster Size: 6 Players

BYOT Teams with rosters less than 6 players will be required to draft additional player to reach 6 players.

Emergency Substitutes will be drawn from the undrafted player pool.

Format

  • 16+ Teams
  • Double Elimination
  • Best of 3 Games
  • Grand Finals Best of 5 Games
